Get better with Kitap

Kitap is the largest bookstore in Kazakhstan, which presents about 5,000 titles of books. Much attention in the assortment is paid to children's literature and educational games for children. The pride of the store is the department of gift and exclusive editions - unique books released in limited editions. The assortment also includes related products, including puzzles, postcards, bookmarks, cartographic publications, souvenirs.
